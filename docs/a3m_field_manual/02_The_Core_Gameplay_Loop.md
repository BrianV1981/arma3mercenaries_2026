# The Core Gameplay Loop

1. **INTERROGATE FOR SEED MONEY**: You deploy with default Squad Leader gear. Detain civilians and escort them to an Interrogation Point to process them for cash. You MUST carry Zip Ties for this.
2. **HIGH VALUE TARGETS (HVTs)**: Keep doing interrogations until you uncover an HVT mission. These targets carry massive amounts of cash (up to 600,000 Cr trigger, 100k on body).
3. **PUSH TO FORT MAGA**: With your wealth, recruit a squad and head to Fort MAGA. Use it as your primary staging area to push into hostile sectors.
4. **EXPAND and LOGISTICS**: You cannot win with just infantry. Logistics and base building are CENTRAL. Use the Quartermaster's Grad Store to build Turrets, Mortars, and defenses.
